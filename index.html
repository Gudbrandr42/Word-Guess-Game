It's Hangman! A Game of Nostalgia

Start typing letters to guess the word and win!

The rules are simple: you have limited guesses. The game is over when you've hung the stick figure.

Guess the right letter, you keep your lives. Guess the wrong letter and you lose a life. Each life lost, draws the gallow and the hungman. Keep playing until you guess the word or lose all your lives. Hit "Play Again" to start over. Have Fun!
